Instructions
  • Use AT&S to prepare formal evaluation(s) for distribution. One evaluation can be used for many sites.
  • In AT&S, prepare a survey set as a "Formal Course Evaluation". You may set the open and due dates, notify on open setting, and results email address(s). You may also set these properties when you distribute to course sites.
  • You may leave the survey unpublished; it will be published when distributed.
  • Use Preview and Test Drive in AT&S to make sure your evaluations are ready.
  • Click on in the list below to distribute a formal evaluation to one or more sites and to add observers.
